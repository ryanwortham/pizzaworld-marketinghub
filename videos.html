Reel Tips & Best Practices

Ideal Length

Keep your reels between 7–15 seconds for maximum engagement. Short, snappy content performs best on TikTok, Instagram Reels, and Facebook. Grab attention in the first 2 seconds!

Best Posting Times

Post weekdays between 6–9 AM or 5–7 PM when your audience is most active. Test different times to find your audience's peak engagement window.

Music & Audio

Use trending audio and music to boost visibility. TikTok and Instagram algorithms favor content with popular sounds. Add captions to keep content engaging with sound off.
